Fasa 1: Potret Dewasa

Prompt Template:

Task: Age Modification.
Input Image: [MUAT NAIK GAMBAR ANDA]
Target Age: 25 years old.

Generate a photorealistic portrait maintaining facial features and ethnicity from the input image. 
Modify structure and skin to realistically represent them as a confident 25-year-old.
Background: Neutral studio background.
Style: 4K, photorealistic.
